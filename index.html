They have hundreds of animals together in a compact space. So, the animals end up pooping and peeing on top of each other. The feces contribute to a positive feedback loop of pollution. The pollution from animal waste consists of lots of methane. Not only does it involve pollution, but also many diseases are easily contracted. This explains why the initial infections of COVID-19 were from an animal marketplace. The living conditions for the animals are crazy.
